Networking Dinner

A networking evening with special guest speaker on:
‘A glimpse into the future: The AI revolution of Healthcare’

December 5th 19:30 – 21:45. Limited seats available.

Welcome cocktail

Abstract

COCIR invites you to a special pre-summit networking dinner with high level representatives from the EU and other influential institutions; government- & regional decision makers and industry. During dinner a leader in Artificial Intelligence will give us a glimpse into the future of how this new powerful technology will revolutionize the way we deliver healthcare - this is an evening not to be missed, book now to secure your place by emailing ehealthsummit@cocir.org.
