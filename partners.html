In Partnership with

INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION FOR INTEGRATED CARE

The International Foundation for Integrated Care (IFIC) is a not-for-profit educational membership-based network that crosses organisational and professional boundaries to bring people together to advance the science, knowledge and adoption of integrated care policy and practice.

https://integratedcarefoundation.org/

INTEGRATED CARE ALLIANCE

The Integrated Care Alliance aims at accelerating the transition towards Integrated Care. We are committed to working together to develop sustainable health and social care systems that meet the holistic needs of citizens, patients and carers, especially those with complex and long-term health and social care needs.

http://www.integratedcarealliance.org/
